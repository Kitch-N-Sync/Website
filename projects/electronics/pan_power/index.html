Project Overview

Another PP special. I needed an easy way to get 120VAC and 24VDC for testing purposes while I was fixing control circuitry for the machines. This is nothing more than a housing, a couple indicators, and some switches I was able to mount to my rolling tool cart. Simple project, but it was fun creating the housing out of the materials we had lying around in scrap bins.
